Dominions 6 Utilities

A collection of calculators and simulators for Dominions 6 mechanics.
  • Spell Casting Simulator
  • Communion Fatigue Calculator
  • Preaching Calculator
  • Collision Calculator
  • Province Report Estimator
  • Patrol & Unrest Calculator

About the Tools:

Spell Casting Simulator: Estimate the probability of one mage finishing their spell sequence before another, considering cast time modifiers, random variation, and Time Stop.

Communion Fatigue Calculator: Simulate fatigue gain and HP loss in communions to predict how long slaves can sustain spellcasting under various conditions.

Preaching Calculator: Calculate the probability distribution for gaining dominion points (candle change) in a province based on priest levels, starting dominion, and temple presence.

Collision Calculator: Simulate unit collisions based on chassis HP to estimate win/loss/slip percentages according to Illwiki mechanics.

Province Report Estimator: Estimate the actual number of units in a province by analyzing numerical scout reports with known inaccuracies (10%, 30%, 40%, 50%) and rounding.

Patrol & Unrest Calculator: Simulate patrol actions to estimate the probability of detecting stealthy units and the effectiveness of unrest reduction.
